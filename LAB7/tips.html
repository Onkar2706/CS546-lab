Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Pack Light

    Traveling with a lighter backpack or suitcase makes your journey more manageable. Only pack essentials and versatile clothing.

  2. Research Local Customs

    Before visiting a new destination, research its customs and etiquette to avoid unintentional disrespect.

  3. Stay in hostels

    They are cheap, organize events, you’ll meet a lot of people, and they are just tons of fun! Plus, hostel bars sell cheap beer. Hostelworld is the best hostel-accommodation site out there, with the largest inventory, best search interface, and highest availability.

  4. Don’t fly direct

    When booking flights, sometimes it is cheaper to fly in to airports close to your final destination, and then take a train, bus, or budget airline to where you need to go.
